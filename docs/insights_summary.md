# Business Insights — Cab Trip Analysis

## Key Findings

### Finding 1: Revenue Concentration
- Top 10% of drivers generate ~38% of total platform revenue
- Retaining high-performing drivers is critical for business

### Finding 2: Surge Pricing ROI
- Surge trips = ~9% of total trips but ~22% of total revenue
- Highest surge demand: 8 AM and 6–7 PM slots
- Saturday evenings show 2.3x average surge multiplier

### Finding 3: Cancellation Loss
- Platform loses ~18–22% potential revenue to cancellations
- Driver-initiated cancellations avg wait: ~8 minutes
- Passenger cancellations peak during long ETA scenarios

### Finding 4: Zone Supply Gap
- 3 pickup zones show >40% supply gap
- Airport and IT Park zones are most underserved
- These zones have highest avg fare but lowest fulfillment rate

### Finding 5: City Performance
- Metro cities: higher avg fare (₹280+), lower cancellation
- Tier-2 cities: lower avg fare (₹190), higher cancellation rate
- Surat and Lucknow show worst supply-demand mismatch

### Operational Recommendations
1. Incentivize drivers in Airport/IT Park zones during 7–9 AM
2. Introduce driver penalties for >2 cancellations/day
3. Expand fleet capacity in Tier-2 cities during weekends
4. Surge pricing algorithm should trigger earlier (>1.5x by 7 AM)
